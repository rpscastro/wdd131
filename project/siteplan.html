Site Name

Rafa's Brazilian Pizzeria

This name represents a Brazilian Pizzeria named after its owner's nickname: Rafa.

Optional domain availability: rafas-brazilian-pizzeria.com

Site Purpose

A generic website to disseminate information about a neighborhood Brazilian pizzeria. Menu, means of payment, delivery options, opening hours, contact information, etc.

Scenarios

What are the pizza flavors available at Rafa's Brazilian Pizzeria?

What are the opening days and hours?

Can I pay using my credit card?

How can I order a pizza?
